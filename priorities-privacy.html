Privacy Policy – Priorities

Last updated: 2 March 2026

Priorities ("the app") is a task-management application that runs entirely on your device.

Data We Collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Task titles
  • Descriptions
  • Due dates
  • Priorities
  • Time-spent records
  • Reminder times

This information is stored locally in an SQLite database on your device and never leaves your device.

Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – to deliver reminders you configure.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM – to trigger reminders at the exact time you set.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – to restore reminders after a device reboot.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE – to keep the task timer running while using other apps.

Third-Party Services

None. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

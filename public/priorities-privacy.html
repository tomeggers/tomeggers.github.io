Privacy Policy — Priorities

Last updated: 2 March 2026

Priorities ("the app") is a task-management application that runs entirely on your device.

Data We Collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Task titles, descriptions, due dates, priorities, time-spent records, and reminder times are stored locally in an SQLite database on your device.
  • This data never leaves your device.

Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: to deliver task reminder notifications you configure.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM: to fire reminders at the exact time you set.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: to restore scheduled alarms after a device reboot.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC: to keep the task timer running while you use other apps.

Third-Party Services

None. No analytics SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, or ad networks are used.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
